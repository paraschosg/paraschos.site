# Privacy notice

## Last updated: June 2026 · Applies to paraschos.site

// Who runs this site

This is the personal portfolio of George Paraschos. For anything privacy-related, email george@paraschos.site.

// What runs only if you allow it

When you first visit, a banner asks whether to enable analytics. If you decline (or never answer), none of the following loads:

  • Google Analytics — anonymized page-view statistics. Google may set cookies. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Visit counter (counterapi.dev) — increments a single number, stores nothing about you.
  • Presence & events (Supabase) — a random session ID (e.g. k3x9f2) to show the "N online" count, plus anonymous events like "projects tab opened". No names, no IP storage on my side, no profiles.

You can change your mind any time:

// What this site stores in your browser

A few values in localStorage, which never leave your device: your theme preference, a local visit count for the "welcome back" message, and your analytics choice. Clearing your browser data removes all of it.

// What this site does NOT do

  • No browser fingerprinting.
  • No advertising or ad networks.
  • No selling or sharing of data with third parties beyond the services named above.
  • No tracking across other websites.

// Third-party requests that are part of the site itself

Regardless of your analytics choice, your browser fetches fonts from Google Fonts and public data from the GitHub API (my recent activity and contributions graph) to render the page. These providers see your IP address as part of serving the request, as with any web resource.

// Your rights

Under the GDPR you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of any data concerning you, and you can lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (dpa.gr). Given how little this site collects, the fastest route is simply emailing me.
